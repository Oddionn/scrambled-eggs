Fluffy Scrambled Eggs

Not to sound full of myself, but I have been told on multiple occasions that my scrambled eggs are some of the best people have ever eaten. I've been tweeking this recipe since i was twenty years old. "Everyone knows how to make scrambled eggs," you might think. True anyone can scramble some eggs, but making fluffy, moist, and cheesy eggs is a work of art. Follow my instructions you wont end up with brown, rubbery brown eggs. You will end up being the star of scrambled eggs for your friends and family. So you want to make tasty eggs at home? Here's how!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Tbs of butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 Tbs of milk
  • 1/4 Tsp of baking powder
  • 1/4 - 1/2 Tsp of salt
  • Fresh ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 Cup of grated cheddar cheese

DIRECTIONS

  1. In a bowl, whisk eggs with milk, baking powder, salt, and black pepper until thoroughly combined. Let set at room tempeature for 5 minutes.
  2. Heat butter until sizzling over medium high heat in a large skillet
  3. Right before adding the eggs to the skillet, whisk in the grated cheese
  4. Pour the eggs into the hot skillet and let sit for atleast 10 seconds. Then begin stirring and scambling with a heat-proof spatula until they have reach desired consistency.(Do not overcook the eggs, they will continue to cook when removed from the skillet!)
